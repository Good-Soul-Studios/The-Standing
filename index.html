They Watch

A psychological horror experience that asks the question: what happens when the ones watching... never leave?

The Story

In a world where society is in shambles, not because of a plague, or zombies, or nuclear war, but because of something mostly unseen that plagues civilization... They nobody remembers where they came from or what They are, but they know to hide, as difficult as it may be As you progress through the game you will find lore on what happened to the world.

You play as Cole, A man with little to know discernable past, to him the world just seems like a big blur, like he can't even understand the simple things he looks at because of Their presence and confusion.
